h3. 源代码中自带的 Hadoop-eclipse 插件无法正常使用，需要自己进行编译。网上的教程让我有太多的眼泪，还是自己写一个吧，哈里路亚

h2. 平台环境

* Win7
* Eclipse Helios(include Ant-1.7)
* Hadoop-1.0.4 

h2. Step1 导入 Hadoop-eclipse 插件工程

<a href="http://archive.apache.org/dist/hadoop/core/hadoop-1.0.4/" target="_blank">获取 hadoop-1.0.4.tar.gz</a>

解压缩到 Eclipse workspace 目录下（如: F:\workspace）

在 Eclipse 中选择 File-->Import-->Existing Projects into Workspace 导入已有项目，路径为: F:\workspace\hadoop-1.0.4\src\contrib\eclipse-plugin，其默认的项目名称是 MapReduceTools

h2. Step2 导入所需 jar 包

在项目 MapReduceTools 中新建 lib 目录，将 hadoop-1.0.4 下的 hadoop-core-1.0.4.jar（重命名为 hadoop.core.jar），及其 lib 目录下的 commons-cli-1.2.jar、commons-lang-2.4.jar、commons-configuration-1.6.jar、jackson-mapper-asl-1.8.8.jar、jackson-core-asl-1.8.8.jar、commons-httpclient-3.0.1.jar 拷贝到该目录。

h2. Step3 build-contrib.xml

将 F:\workspace\hadoop-1.0.4\src\contrib\build-contrib.xml（与 eclipse-plugin 包在同一级目录）拷贝到 MapReduceTools 项目目录中。

修改 build-contrib.xml，注意路径用 "/"

<!-- 修改 hadoop 存放目录-->
%{color:#a52713}<property name="hadoop.root" location="F:/workspace/hadoop-1.0.4"/>%
<!-- 添加 eclipse 存放目录-->
%{color:#a52713}<property name="eclipse.home" location="F:/eclipse" />%
<!-- 添加 hadoop 版本-->
%{color:#a52713}<property name="version" value="1.0.4"/>%

h2. Step4 build.xml

修改 build.xml

<!-- 1.修改 build-contrib.xml 的位置，去掉"../"-->
%{color:#a52713}<import file="build-contrib.xml"/>%

<!-- 2.添加如下，否则提示"软件包 org.apache.hadoop.fs 不存在"-->
%{color:#a52713}<path id="hadoop-jars">%
%{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;<fileset dir="${hadoop.root}/">%
%{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<include name="hadoop-*.jar" />%
%{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;</fileset>%
%{color:#a52713}</path>%

<path id="classpath">
&nbsp;&nbsp;<pathelement location="${build.classes}"/>
&nbsp;&nbsp;<pathelement location="${hadoop.root}/build/classes"/>
&nbsp;&nbsp;<path refid="eclipse-sdk-jars"/>
%{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;<path refid="hadoop-jars"/>%
</path>

<!-- 3.修改并添加 jar 包拷贝-->
<target name="jar" depends="compile" unless="skip.contrib">
&nbsp;&nbsp;<mkdir dir="${build.dir}/lib"/>
%{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;<copy file="${hadoop.root}/hadoop-core-${version}.jar" tofile="${build.dir}/lib/hadoop-core.jar" verbose="true"/>%
%{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;<copy file="${hadoop.root}/lib/commons-cli-1.2.jar" todir="${build.dir}/lib" verbose="true"/>%
%{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;<copy file="${hadoop.root}/lib/commons-lang-2.4.jar" todir="${build.dir}/lib" verbose="true"/>%
%{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;<copy file="${hadoop.root}/lib/commons-configuration-1.6.jar" todir="${build.dir}/lib" verbose="true"/>%
%{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;<copy file="${hadoop.root}/lib/jackson-mapper-asl-1.8.8.jar" todir="${build.dir}/lib" verbose="true"/>%
%{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;<copy file="${hadoop.root}/lib/jackson-core-asl-1.8.8.jar" todir="${build.dir}/lib" verbose="true"/>%
%{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;<copy file="${hadoop.root}/lib/commons-httpclient-3.0.1.jar" todir="${build.dir}/lib" verbose="true"/>%
%{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;<jar jarfile="${build.dir}/hadoop-${name}-${version}.jar" manifest="${root}/META-INF/MANIFEST.MF">%
%{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<fileset dir="${build.dir}" includes="classes/ lib/"/>%
%{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<fileset dir="${root}" includes="resources/ plugin.xml"/>%
%{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;</jar>%
</target>

h2. Step5 MANIFEST.MF

修改 META-INF/MANIFEST.MF

Bundle-ClassPath: classes/,
 &nbsp;&nbsp;lib/hadoop-core.jar %{color:#a52713},%
 %{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;lib/commons-cli-1.2.jar,%
 %{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;lib/commons-httpclient-3.0.1.jar,%
 %{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;lib/jackson-core-asl-1.8.8.jar,%
 %{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;lib/jackson-mapper-asl-1.8.8.jar,%
 %{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;lib/commons-configuration-1.6.jar,%
 %{color:#a52713}&nbsp;&nbsp;lib/commons-lang-2.4.jar%

h2. Step6 Ant 编译

右键 MapReduceTools 工程，Properties-->Builders-->New-->Ant Builder
